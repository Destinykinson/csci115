Fabriana Updates

Fabriana is a Boa Constrictor we've had for several years. She is the snake we've had the longest. She is ~10 years old and between 8.5 and 9 feet long. Four years ago, she gave birth to 23 babies. She's very active and always moving around her enclosure.
